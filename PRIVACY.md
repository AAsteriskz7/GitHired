# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 23, 2026

GitHired ("the Extension") takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains what information our extension collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## Information Collection and Use

The Extension interacts with Google and GitHub job aggregator tables. When you mark a job as "Applied," "Skipped," or "Pending," that selection is saved to your browser's local storage utilizing the `chrome.storage` API. This allows the extension to remember your preferences and appropriately highlight job listings upon your return.

## Data Storage and Privacy

*   **Local Storage Only:** All data generated by your interactions with the Extension is stored locally onto your device.
*   **No Data Transmission:** The Extension **does not** transmit, upload, or collect any personal data, usage data, tracking information, or analytics off of your device. 
*   **Third-Party Sharing:** Furthermore, the developer does not share, sell, or rent any of your data to any third parties whatsoever, as the developer does not have access to any of this data in the first place.

## Your Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to our privacy policy.

## Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. All updates will be effective immediately upon posting.

## Contact
